Our Story

A legacy of craftsmanship from the Shoe Capital of the Philippines.

From Marikina, For the World

Bristol Shoes is a proudly Filipino brand rooted in Marikina City. With dedicated craftsmanship since the late 1970s, Bristol continues to deliver handcrafted shoes for men and women.

Each pair is made by skilled artisans using the finest cow and pigskin leathers.
