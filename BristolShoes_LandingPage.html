Our Story

Marikina's
Finest
Footwear

Bristol Shoes & Shoe Factory is a proudly Filipino brand rooted in the shoemaking capital of the Philippines — Marikina City. For over four decades, we've been crafting premium leather footwear that balances timeless elegance with everyday comfort.

Each pair is handcrafted by skilled local artisans using the finest cow and pig skin leathers, sourced both locally and internationally to ensure uncompromising quality in every stitch.
